Form Example Positive Negative Question
Regular verbs (-ed) walked, talked, played I walked to the store. I didn't walk to the store. Did you walk to the store?
Irregular verbs went, ate, saw I went to the park. I didn't go to the park. Did you go to the park?
Verbs ending in -y studied, carried, tried I studied for my exam. I didn't study for my exam. Did you study for your exam?
Verbs ending in -e lived, loved, liked I lived in Paris for a year. I didn't live in Paris for a year. Did you live in Paris for a year?
Verbs ending in a consonant + a vowel + a consonant stopped, planned, begged I stopped at the red light. I didn't stop at the red light. Did you stop at the red light?
Verbs with irregular past tense and past participle forms was/were, had, did I was at home yesterday. I wasn't at home yesterday. Were you at home yesterday?
Phrasal verbs turned on, took off, looked for I turned on the light. I didn't turn on the light. Did you turn on the light?
